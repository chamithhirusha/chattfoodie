Creamy Pasley Salad

Megan yen

About

It seems like there might be a typo in your request ("Pasley" instead of "Parsley"). Assuming you're looking for a Creamy Parsley Salad recipe, here's a delicious and simple version for you

Ingredients

1 bunch fresh parsley, finely chopped

1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved

1 cucumber, diced

1/2 red onion, thinly sliced

1/2 cup Greek yogurt

2 tablespoons mayonnaise

2 tablespoons sour cream

2 tablespoons lemon juice

1 clove garlic, minced

Instructions

1

Chop parsley, halve tomatoes, dice cucumber, slice red onion.

Mix yogurt, mayonnaise, sour cream, lemon juice, garlic, salt, and pepper.

2
3

Toss parsley, tomatoes, cucumber, and onion in a bowl.

Pour dressing over vegetables.

4
5

Mix until coated.

Chill if desired, then serve.
