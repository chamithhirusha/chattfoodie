Chilie Crabs

Jason moory

About

Chilli Crab is a popular and delicious seafood dish that originated in Singapore. It's a flavorful and spicy crab dish known for its rich, tangy, and slightly sweet sauce. Chilli Crab is a delightful and messy dish, often enjoyed with hands or using crab crackers. The spicy and tangy sauce complements the sweet crab meat, making it a favorite among seafood lovers. Here's a general description of how to make Chilli Crab

Ingredients

2 large crabs, cleaned and cut into pieces

2 tablespoons vegetable oil

1 onion, finely chopped

4 cloves garlic, minced

2 tablespoons chili paste (according to your taste)

1 cup tomato ketchup

1/2 cup sweet chili sauce

1/4 cup soy sauce

2 tablespoons oyster sauce

1 tablespoon sugar

Salt and pepper to taste

Green onions, chopped (for garnish)

Fresh coriander, chopped (for garnish)

Lime wedges (for serving)

Instructions

Clean the crabs thoroughly, removing the shells and gills. Cut the crabs into pieces, making it easier to eat.
