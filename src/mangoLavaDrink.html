Mango lava drink

Micella tim

About

Mango lava drink is a delightful and refreshing beverage that combines the tropical sweetness of mangoes with a touch of indulgent creaminess. This exotic drink is not only visually appealing but also a treat for your taste buds. Here's a simple recipe for making a delicious Mango Lava Drink

Calories

60%

Protein

40%

Fat

30%

Carbohydrates

50%

Ingredients

1 cup ripe mango, peeled and diced

1 cup mango juice

1/2 cup vanilla ice cream

1/2 cup milk

2 tablespoons honey or sugar (adjust according to taste)

Ice cubes (optional)

Whipped cream for garnish (optional)

Mango slices for garnish (optional)

Instructions

1

Take the diced ripe mango and blend it in a blender or food processor until you get a smooth puree.

In the blender, combine the mango puree, mango juice, vanilla ice cream, milk, and honey (or sugar).

2
3

Blend the mixture until it reaches a smooth and creamy consistency. If you prefer a colder drink, you can add ice cubes and blend again.

Pour the mango lava drink into glasses.

4
5

Top the drink with a dollop of whipped cream for an extra indulgent touch.Garnish with mango slices on the rim of the glass or as a decoration on top.

Serve the Mango Lava Drink immediately and enjoy the tropical flavors and creamy texture.
